## Lorentz transformation

$$
\begin{align}
x' &= \frac{x-ut/c^2}{\sqrt{1-u^2/c^2}} \\
t' &= \frac{t-ux/c^2}{\sqrt{1-u^2/c^2}}\\
y' &= y \\
z' &= z
\end{align}
$$

## Rotation

$$
\begin{align} \label{rotation}
x' &= x\cos(\theta) + y\sin(\theta) \\
y' &= y\cos(\theta) - x\sin(\theta) \\
z' &= z \\
t' &= t
\end{align}
$$
